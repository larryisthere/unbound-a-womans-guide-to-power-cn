# About the Author | 关于作者

Kasia Urbaniak is the founder and CEO of The Academy, a school that teaches women the foundations of power and influence.

卡西亚·乌尔班尼亚克是学院的创始人和首席执行官，这是一所教授女性力量和影响力基础的学校。

Urbaniak's perspective on power is unique. Over the course of nearly twenty years, she has worked as professional dominatrix, practiced Taoist alchemy in one of the oldest female-led monasteries in China, and obtained dozens of certifications in different disciplines, including Medical Qigong and Systemic Constellations.

乌尔班尼亚克对力量的视角是独特的。在近二十年的时间里，她曾作为专业女支配者工作，在中国最古老的女性主导的修道院之一实践道教炼金术，并在不同学科获得了数十个认证，包括医疗气功和系统排列。

Since founding The Academy in 2013, Urbaniak has taught hundreds of women practical tools to step into leadership positions in their relationships, families, workplaces, and wider communities. She has spoken at corporations and conferences worldwide. Her students include artists and politicians, philanthropists and aid workers, lawyers and judges, as well as executives from some of the biggest corporations in America, including Amazon, Google, and the major banks. She has counseled Oscar-winning film producers and Oscar- and Tony award–winning performers and directors and has spoken at the Yale School of Management, at Wharton School of business, and at the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA), as well as at conferences worldwide.

自2013年创立学院以来，乌尔班尼亚克已经教授了数百名女性实用工具，让她们在关系、家庭、工作场所和更广泛的社区中担任领导职位。她在世界各地的公司和会议上发表演讲。她的学生包括艺术家和政治家、慈善家和援助工作者、律师和法官，以及来自美国一些最大公司的高管，包括亚马逊、谷歌和主要银行。她曾为奥斯卡获奖电影制片人和奥斯卡及托尼奖获奖表演者和导演提供咨询，并曾在耶鲁管理学院、沃顿商学院和现代艺术博物馆（MoMA）发表演讲，以及在世界各地的会议上发表演讲。
